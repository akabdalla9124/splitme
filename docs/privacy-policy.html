Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

SplitMe ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use the SplitMe iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

  • Email address and password (used for authentication via Firebase)
  • Display name and username you choose
  • Profile photo (if you choose to upload one)

Content you create

  • Bill splits: restaurant name, date, items, amounts, and the people involved
  • Dining experiences: restaurant name, rating, notes, and optional photos
  • Restaurant ratings and reviews

Technical information

  • Device push notification token (for friend request and split update notifications)
  • Photos from your camera or library, only when you explicitly choose to attach them

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve the SplitMe experience:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To store and display your bill splits, dining experiences, and ratings
  • To enable the friends system — finding friends by username or email and sharing experiences
  • To send push notifications for friend requests and split activity (only if you grant permission)
  • To power the AI receipt scanning feature (images are sent to Anthropic's API for processing and are not stored by us)
  • To display restaurant information via the Google Places API

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore database and Firebase Storage), which provides industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Profile photos and receipt images are stored in Firebase Storage. We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

4. Third-Party Services

SplitMe uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, and file storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Places API — restaurant search and details. Google Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI-powered receipt image analysis. Images sent for parsing are not stored by Anthropic beyond the request. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Expo (EAS) — app distribution and push notifications. Expo Privacy Policy

5. Camera and Photo Library

SplitMe requests access to your camera and photo library only when you actively use features that require it: scanning a receipt, adding a photo to a dining experience, or updating your profile picture. We do not access your camera or photos in the background.

6. Push Notifications

We may send push notifications to inform you of friend requests and activity on splits you're involved in. You can disable notifications at any time through your iPhone's Settings app.

7. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data upon request

To delete your account or request your data, please contact us at the email below. Account deletion removes all your profile data, splits, dining experiences, and ratings from our servers.

8. Children's Privacy

SplitMe is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of SplitMe after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
